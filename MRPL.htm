Instrumentation and Control Department

The Instrumentation and Control Department at MRPL plays a pivotal role in ensuring safe, efficient, and reliable plant operations by monitoring and controlling various process parameters.

Key Responsibilities

1. Sensing Parameters:

Key parameters monitored include pressure, flow, level, and temperature. Other variables like pH, electrical conductivity, and density fall under analytical variables.

Sensors are chosen based on the environment and are maintained to ensure accurate readings. The data from these sensors is sent to the control room for continuous monitoring by the operating engineers.

2. Process Control:

The department ensures that all process variables are maintained within prescribed limits by applying control algorithms like PID (Proportional-Integral-Derivative) on Distributed Control Systems (DCS).

The final control is often executed through valves, ensuring that operational parameters are set and adjusted as needed.

3. Safety and Maintenance:

Instrumentation engineers must ensure the safe operation of the plant by maintaining sensors and control systems and following safety protocols during installation and maintenance activities.

Through continuous monitoring and process control, the Instrumentation Department ensures that MRPL operates efficiently and safely, contributing to the overall productivity and operational excellence of the refinery.
